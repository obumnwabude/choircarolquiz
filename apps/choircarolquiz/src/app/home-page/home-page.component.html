About Quiz

  • Compete with other participants of the Christmas Cantata Quiz.
  • Answer timed multiple choice questions online on the following Bible passages:
    • Isaiah chapters 7 to 11
    • Luke chapters 1 to 4
  • Compete with other participants in 3 rounds.
  • Score 50% and above in the first round to progress to the second round.
  • Top 5 participants (champions) of the second round's leaderboard progress to the final round.
  • Champions compete on stage during the Christmas Cantata for winners to emerge.
